Who We Are

NBCS is an environmental firm that seamlessly merges innovation and functionality to redefine environmental compliance and sustainability. We want to make sure that our stated values do not classify as lip service—we want them to be felt in every project that we deliver and in each milestone that we accomplish. We believe that there is a greater need for faithfully adhering to environmental-compliance and not just compliance for the sake of it.

Our Services

  • Environmental & Resource Consulting

    Covering natural resources, environmental assessment, hydrogeological studies, and environmental monitoring for project planning and site evaluation.

  • Engineering & Waste Management Solutions

    Providing wastewater engineering, solid waste management, treatment system design, and construction facilitation for sustainable project implementation.

  • Regulatory & Community Support Services

    Handling ECC/CNC and permit processing, compliance reporting, public scoping, IEC, training, and stakeholder engagement.
